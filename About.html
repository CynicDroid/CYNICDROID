about CynicDroid Technologies

Who are we?

CynicDroid Technologies is a firm specialized in designing and implementing information systems (IT) . As systems analysts we assess the suitability of information systems in terms of their intended outcomes and liaise with end users, software vendors and programmers in order to achieve these outcomes. We use analysis and design techniques to solve business problems using information technology. We may also serve as change agents who identify the organizational improvements needed, design systems to implement those changes, and train and motivate others to use the systems.

What do we do?

We try to offer a variety of services including but not limited to:
• Analyzing, designing, coding and implementing computer programs providing user support.
• Configuring of new Information Communication Technology equipment.
• Repairing and maintaining of Information Communication Technology equipment and associated peripherals.
• Install and configure software and hardware for new and used computers to make them ready for use.
• Designing, coding and deployment of websites providing user support.
• Android devices software maintenance.
• Windows and Android platform data recovery.
• Offering internet services i.e emails and web conferencing. 

Our Mission

* To offer support to our softwares.
* To provide documented softwares.
* To secure our packages and develop using the best of the installer packaging system.
* To advice accordingly.
* To respond to our helpline forum instantly.
* To ensure Data security is our key component.
* To liaise with end users, software vendors and programmers in order to achieve the best we can offer.
* To offer pocket friendly products and services.
